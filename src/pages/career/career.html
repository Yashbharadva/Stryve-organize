Why Stryve?

We try not to limit ourselves to 'perks and benefits', but here a few of the things that people love about working at Stryve…

Team away days

From team-building days to experiential activities, we love getting away from it all to reset and energise as a group.

Free gym membership

We have a gym on site, as well as showers and bike storage. So whether you're an avid gym-goer or a casual cyclist, we have something for everyone.

Work to live

Sure - work is important. But so is having a happy and balanced life. We offer flexible working, from fully remote to hybrid - whatever suits your lifestyle!

Become a Stryve shareholder

We want everyone to push for greatness with Stryve, which is why we offer all new joiners the opportunity to be part of our EMI share scheme.
